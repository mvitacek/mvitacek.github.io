What is a portfolio?

An art portfolio is a curated collection of an individual's best and most representative artworks, showcasing their unique style, skills, creativity, and artistic evolution

What not to include

  • Master Copies
  • Fan Art
  • Speed Painting (time lapses)
  • Screen Plays/Scripts
  • Art Assignments
  • Architecture
  • Cliche Photography
  • Works with obvious ties to reference photos you did not take yourself (e.i. Pinterest or stock photos)

Tips and Tricks

  • Start Early!
  • Go to Portfolio Reviews
  • Pre College programs and classes at SAIC outside of school are a great way to gain pieces
  • Colleges are looking for variety. Push yourself out of your comfort zone!
